    FAQ

    How can I open an account?

    First thing to do is to register. Go to the Homepage and click the login button at the top of page. Navigate to the sign up page and create an account. After successfully creating a user account. Go back to Homepage, scroll down to middle of the page and click the get started button.

    Can my account be dormant?

    Yes, your account can be dormant if not used.
